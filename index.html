Start a cycling plan!

STATrider helps you find events,

set a goal, track your progress

and analyze your data.


Events

STATrider helps you find a cycling event to be your main objective for the season.

You can search through different databases to find one or more events to add to your favorites and see how they will fit into your cycling calendar.

Sometimes event organizers have difficulty formalizing dates in the early season, that is why you can search races in past seasons and simulate dates for the current year.

Just don't forget to check the event website later during the season to update your plan.

Setup

Selected one event to be your major goal for the season and STATrider will create a calendar for your cycling training.

You can also customize the number of weeks for each training block and set boundaries for your Body Mass Index during the season.

Calendar

STATrider calendar will give you an overview of your planning, broken down into weeks, where you can customize the planned hours and track your progress.

Targets

Set weekly targets for key performance variables, track them with very simple charts, and analyse it.

Weight

You can record your weight every week, compare with previous seasons and keep track of one.

Load

Load data from your rides to track and analyze your progress. We are currently supporting reports exported from connect.garmin.com. For more details, check our Support page.

Dashboard

Keep track of your progress and upcoming events with charts showing you key performance variables to achieve your goal.

Analytics

Get insights from your data with fun facts and customizable charts all in one-screen to help you have that eureka moment!
