Initial Setup

Opening STATrider for the first time, you'll see a security warning in a message bar as shown below.

In order to make STATrider fully functional you'll have to click Enable Content.

On your Events first search, you'll see a pop up as shown below. Please make sure to check the box Ignore Privacy levels and click on Save.


Load Data From Your Garmin

1. Open a web browser and go to connect.garmin.com.

2. On your left corner click on Reports.

3. On Reports menu click on Progress Summary.

4. Click on Filter by and select Group by Week.

5. On your right corner click on Customize and make sure you have all necessary fields checked:

  • Activities
  • Average Speed
  • Total Distance
  • Average Heart Rate
  • Total Activity Time
  • Average Power
  • Activity Calories
  • Average Bike Cadence
  • Total Elev Gain
